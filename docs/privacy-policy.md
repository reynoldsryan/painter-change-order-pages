# Painter Change Order Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

Painter Change Order is a local-first iPhone app for residential painters creating client-ready extra work requests from job-site photos, a short scope note, and simple price and time impact fields.

## Data Collection

Painter Change Order does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, a backend service, or cloud sync.

## Data Stored On Your Device

Painter Change Order stores the request photos, scope notes, price and time impact entries, saved defaults, Pro unlock state, and local request history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties.

## Permissions

Painter Change Order may request photo library access so you can attach job-site photos to a request.

The app does not request camera, microphone, contacts, location, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Painter Change Order offers an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Data Deletion

Because data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
